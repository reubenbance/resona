The Challenge

Traditional fundraising platforms aren't built for distributed, volunteer-led campaigns. Organisations face significant barriers:

  • High platform fees that eat into precious donations
  • Complex tools that volunteers struggle to use effectively
  • Limited visibility into individual fundraiser performance
  • Lack of real-time tracking and leaderboards to motivate teams
  • Poor integration with existing websites and communication tools
