The Challenge

Traditional fundraising platforms aren't built for distributed, volunteer-led campaigns. Organisations face significant barriers:

  • High platform fees that eat into smaller donations
  • Complex tools that volunteers struggle to use effectively
  • Limited visibility into individual fundraiser performance
  • Lack of real-time tracking and leaderboards to motivate teams
  • Poor integration with existing websites and communication tools

Our Solution

Resona provides lightweight, embeddable micro-giving tools designed specifically for nonprofits, charities, and political campaigns running grassroots campaigns.

Individual Tracking

Each fundraiser gets a unique identifier. Every donation is automatically attributed to the right person, giving you complete visibility into your grassroots network.

Live Leaderboards & Maps

Beautiful, embeddable widgets that show real-time fundraising progress. From competitive leaderboards to collaborative maps, we make the data engaging and motivating.

Seamless Integration

Works with your existing payment infrastructure. Simple dashboards that embed anywhere. Automated data sync with no complex setup required. No extra platforms to remember!

Cost-Effective

Built on top of industry-standard payment processors like Stripe. You only pay standard processing fees - no additional platform charges eating into your mission.

Mobile-First Design

All our tools are fully responsive and optimised for mobile devices, ensuring your fundraisers and donors have a seamless experience on any screen.

Quick Deployment

Go live in days, not months. Our tools are designed to integrate quickly with minimal technical overhead, so you can focus on your campaign.

How It Works

Getting started with Resona is straightforward. We handle the technical complexity so you can focus on your mission.

1

Connect Your Payment System

We integrate with your Stripe account (or similar) to track donations in real-time.

2

Assign Fundraiser IDs

Each volunteer receives a unique identifier that links their donations to your tracking system.

3

Embed the Widgets

Copy and paste our interactive dashboards into your website. We customise the look and feel to match your brand.

4

Watch It Grow

Monitor performance in real-time. Your team sees live updates, keeping everyone motivated and engaged.

About Resona

Reuben Bance

Founder, Resona Consulting Ltd

I founded Resona to solve a problem I witnessed firsthand: mission-driven organisations struggling to harness technology for grassroots fundraising campaigns.

With a background in data analysis and nonprofit work, I saw how the right tools could transform on-the-ground fundraising. But existing platforms were either too expensive, too complex, or simply not designed for volunteer-led efforts.

Resona bridges that gap with simple, affordable micro-giving technology that empowers organisations to focus on what matters most: their mission.
